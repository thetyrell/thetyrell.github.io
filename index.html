Privacy Cat Graph & Blocker 🐾

Your Feline Guardian for Web Privacy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Privacy Cat Graph & Blocker. We believe that privacy tools should be transparent, effective, and local. This policy outlines how we handle data—which, quite simply, is that we don't.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Privacy Cat is a Local-Only extension. This means:

  • No Data Transmission: We do not send your browsing history, IP address, or personal info to any external servers.
  • No Account Required: You never have to sign up or provide an email to use the extension.
  • No Tracking: We don't track how you use the extension. We are here to stop trackers, not join them.

3. Permissions Explained

To guard your privacy, Privacy Cat requires specific technical permissions:

  • webRequest: Allows the Cat to "see" which third-party domains are trying to ping your browser so we can show them on your graph.
  • declarativeNetRequest: The "claws" of the extension. This allows us to block tracking scripts before they load.
  • storage: Used to remember your "Blocker ON/OFF" settings locally on your computer.

4. Third-Party Services

Privacy Cat does not share data with any third parties. All data processing for the visual graph happens entirely within your browser session and is wiped when the session ends.

5. Contact

If you have questions about how Privacy Cat protects you, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support link or our project repository.
